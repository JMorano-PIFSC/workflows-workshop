# Sandwiches
## My favorite sandwich
My *favorite* sandwich to order is the **reuben**. 

Ingredients:
- [Corned beef](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corned_beef) (alternatively, [pastrami](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pastrami))
- Swiss cheese
- Sauerkraut
- Russian or Thousand Island dressing
- Rye bread

Let's slice it 3 ways $0.33 = 1 / 3$

Or we can slice it to feed any number of people.
$$
X = 1 sandwich / N people
$$
